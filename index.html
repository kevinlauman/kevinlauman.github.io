Hello! My name is Kevin and I am a lover of all things outdoors. I particularly enjoy exploring the backcountry and experiencing nature in its raw form. The solitude and peace that comes with being away from civilization is something I treasure. When I'm not out in the wilderness, I enjoy woodworking and creating beautiful pieces of furniture or other crafts..

I am currently seeking my next career adventure as a Scrum Master or Agile Coach.
